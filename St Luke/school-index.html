          WELCOME TO St. Luke Catholic School

          St. Luke School is a parish school committed to a quality, faithful education based in Catholic values and traditions. St. Luke has served the north side of Indianapolis for over fifty years. St. Luke School is a National Blue Ribbon School, and has achieved both a 4-Star and "A" rating by the Indiana Department of Education.
