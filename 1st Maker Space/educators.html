Inspiring STEAM Education in Indianapolis Area Schools

a maker’s recipe for success:

What is a makerspace? Makerspaces are spaces where students can gather to create, invent, and learn.
Who is 1st Makerspace? We are an organization that supports and empowers students to facilitate meaningful
learning experiences. 1st Makerspace connects formal & informal learning by providing hands-on “Making”
opportunities that enrich and add value to their education in the STEAM AREAS:

Levels of Engagement:

Build a Makerspace

If your school would like full access to machinery such as laser cutters, 3D printers, welding, and other industrial equipment, 1st Makerspace can build an lab for you. Upon completion of the lab, outside of the calendar school year, 1st Makerspace will have unlimited access to the space within the school.

become a host school:

A host school offers a space in their school to host eight week summer camps in addition to the commitment level of placing 20 students in these camps. Host school’s receive this added benefit to their school.

loan a 3d printer

A school that wishes to receive a 3D printer for their academic year can apply below. The only requirement for these schools is to find 20 students to attend summer camps throughout the 1st Makerspace summer camp program.
