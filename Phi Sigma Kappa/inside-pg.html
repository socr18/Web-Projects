IOWA CHAPTER DEDICATION

FIND YOUR HOME AWAY FROM HOME.

As you know, Phi Sigma Kappa gives its undergraduate brothers a community, a home, and a unique college experience, but that's not where it ends. Phi Sigma Kappa is here to join you on your collegiate journey, and we want to assist wherever we can.

Check out the scholarships we offer, our career center, and the leadership training you can obtain only through Phi Sig. For the officers, check out the forms/procedures section to ensure you're fulfilling the requirements of your chapter. Take a look at our directories to find undergraduate and alumni brothers near you, or visit our staff page, and contact the IHQ if there is anything we can assist you with.

It is an honor and a privilege to be a Phi Sig ... Damn Proud!
