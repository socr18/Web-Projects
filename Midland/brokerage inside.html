Subtitle about Midland Atlantic

Midland Atlantic is a full-service real estate firm covering the Midwest and Mid-Atlantic regions dedicated to finding creative and effective solutions to brokerage and development projects. Our combination of tremendous industry experience and true dedication to serving our clients places us at the forefront of our industry.

Midland Atlantic employs more than 30 brokers, developers, property managers and support staff in its offices in Cincinnati, Indianapolis and Raleigh, NC.
